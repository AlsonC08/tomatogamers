GREEN PEA APOCALYPSE V3.0 | awesome-llama

Shooting: Press space to shoot the pistol, press B to reload the pistol (7 bullet per reload) , press G to throw grenade, press V to reload the grenade (1 grenade per reload), press M to shoot the semi-auto (buy it in the shop), and C to reload (7 wave of bullet per reload) press X to shoot the shotgun (buy it in the shop), and Z to reload (two shotgun shells per reload) also if you press N it will use up a first aid pack (they will spawn randomly around the map, or you can buy them in the shop). Shop: click on the shop to open it, (from left to right) item one is First Aid Pack cost:1 coin (first aid plus one), item two is Advance Bullets cost:3 coins (increase range and accuracy(permanently)),item three is auto rocket cost:6 deploy a rocket every six second, item four it is the Semi-auto cost:8 coins (semi-auto permanently), item five Shockwave cost:9 coins (Shock wave will activate every few second, also chance of shooting shockwave bullet permanently) item six is body amour cost:4 coins (give resistant to toxic permanently)item seven is the shotgun cost: 7 coins (shotgun permanently) Max coin capacity:10 coins
