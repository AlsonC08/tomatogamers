DUEL | --Accension--

Instructions Click Play to start the game (it will switch to a random stage if none is selected) If you click Stages, it brings up a stage menu. Click one to set the stage. Then click play to start with that stage. Note: there is a special stage that you have a small chance of getting, so be on the lookout for that! It will be quite recognizable, I can tell you that. Controls: P1 (Red) WASD to move, S to shoot or use powerup when in mid-air from jump. P1 (Blue) Arrow keys to move, down arrow to shoot or use powerup when in mid-air from jump. Powerups: Orbital Strike: calls in a massive laser on the enemy's position. It's an insta-KO if it hits, but takes a little time to charge up. Sniper Shot: Fires a bullet in the direction of the enemy. It's an insta-KO as well, but is fairly easy to dodge. Camo: Turns the player white (the color of the background), allowing you to escape or get a few good hits in.
